Overview

The Lenovo ThinkPad X240 is a powerful and ultra-portable laptop designed for business professionals. With its sturdy build, excellent keyboard, and strong performance, it's the perfect companion for on-the-go productivity.

Specifications

  • Processor: Intel Core i5 (4th Gen)
  • RAM: Up to 8GB DDR3
  • Storage: 128GB/256GB SSD
  • Display: 12.5" HD / Full HD
  • Battery Life: Up to 10 hours
  • Weight: Approx. 1.42kg
  • Ports: USB 3.0, Mini DisplayPort, Ethernet, SD Card Slot
